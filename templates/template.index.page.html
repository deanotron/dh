FAQ

Does Folio include a domain and hosting?

Folio is a self-hosted application, which allows you to choose your hosting provider. In our premium package we take care of all of this for you.

Does it use WordPress?

Folio is installable as a WordPress theme but we’ve swapped out the overly-complex admin interface with a minimal, tightly-coupled solution. You won’t even know you’re using WordPress.

Why choose a self-hosted solution over monthly portfolio services?

It's your work, you should be able to own every aspect of it. With Folio your work cannot be censored or filtered and you are never locked in with a single company.

Is there a guide for managing content?

Yes, please download our guided walkthrough PDF.

How will I know when updates are available?

Folio is setup to automatically update to the latest version. Check your update status from the settings page.

How is payment handled?

Payments are made with Stripe and your data is never stored on our servers. They accept all major credit cards.

Currently, you can change the colours and fonts. We will be adding the capability to add images and logos to the title page shortly

How do I install Folio on WordPress?

It takes less than 30 seconds from the theme manager. View the install guide here

How do I measure traffic to my website?

Folio supports Google Analytics integration to give you an overview of all the traffic coming into your portfolio.

Are there bulk licensing options for developers or agencies?

Yes, there are options for affiliates and bulk licensing, email us for info on setting up or reselling multiple sites.

What browsers are supported?

Folio has been designed to work with modern browsers, phones, and tablets. Problems may be encountered on older versions of Internet Explorer.
